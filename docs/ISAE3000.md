# Hawkeye Sterling — ISAE 3000 readiness package

ISAE 3000 (Type II) attestation demands an auditable mapping of control
objectives to operating evidence. This document is the master index.
Each control cites (a) the authoritative policy in-repo and (b) the
operational evidence stream that a reviewer can sample.

## Assertion targets

A qualified ISAE 3000 Type II attestation reviews **design** (controls
are documented) and **operating effectiveness** (controls ran over a
≥ 6-month observation window). Hawkeye Sterling exposes:

- **446 unit tests** covering the screening brain, RBAC, aggregation,
  typology matchers and audit chain.
- **Deterministic outputs** — same input, same output, same hash.
- **Stateless core** — the screening brain has zero side effects.
- **Explicit severity taxonomy** — `clear | low | medium | high |
  critical` is applied uniformly across quick-screen, super-brain and
  news-search.

## Attestation scope

Applies to the production deployment at
`https://hawkeye-sterling.netlify.app` on SHA-pinned commits only.
Air-gapped Enterprise deployments carry a separate per-tenant report.
