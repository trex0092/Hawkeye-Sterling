1. PURPOSE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document presents the quarterly jurisdiction heatmap derived from
the firm's counterparty register as at 31 March 2026. It is produced
to support the MLRO's assessment of geographic concentration risk
across the firm's counterparty population. The heatmap is not a risk
assessment in its own right. It is a data input for the MLRO's
professional judgement.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. OVERALL OBSERVATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

The counterparty register at 31 March 2026 contains 87 active
counterparties across 14 jurisdictions. Geographic concentration
remains dominated by the United Arab Emirates, which accounts for 62
of the 87 counterparties (71 per cent). The remaining 25 counterparties
are distributed across 13 jurisdictions. The compliance function does
not identify a material change in geographic concentration from the
previous quarter.

4. JURISDICTIONS OF HIGHEST CONCERN
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. UAE, Ras Al Khaimah. Two critical-rated counterparties and one high-
   rated counterparty, with cross-entity exposure on two. The critical
   ratings relate to the Al Rashid Metal Recyclers counterparty and the
   Atlas Gold FZE counterparty. Both are under active review.

2. UAE, Ajman. One critical-rated counterparty (Al Noor Gold Recycling)
   with cross-entity exposure touching Entity Delta and Entity Echo.
   The counterparty is under review in connection with the recycled
   gold red flag series.

3. Switzerland. Two high-rated counterparties (Precious Refining AG
   and one other), both with cross-entity exposure touching two
   programmes. The exposure is consistent with the firm's refining
   supply chain and is not new this quarter.

4. Panama. One high-rated counterparty with an under-review status.
   The counterparty was added to the register in February 2026 and
   is being assessed under Enhanced Due Diligence.

5. Singapore. One high-rated counterparty (Silk Road Commodities Ltd)
   with cross-entity exposure on Entity Foxtrot. The counterparty is
   under monitoring but not currently under active review.
