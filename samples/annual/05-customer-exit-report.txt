1. PURPOSE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This report lists every counterparty on the firm's counterparty register
whose status was changed to "cleared" (relationship ended by mutual
agreement or natural expiry) or "escalated" (relationship terminated by
the firm on compliance grounds) during the calendar year 2025. It is
prepared to support the MLRO's annual review of customer exits and to
maintain a permanent record of the circumstances under which each
relationship ended.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. POPULATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

During the calendar year 2025 the firm exited or escalated 6
counterparty relationships.

Relationships ended by mutual agreement (cleared):     4
Relationships terminated on compliance grounds
  (escalated):                                         2
                                                       --
Total exits during 2025:                               6

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. ENTRIES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

COUNTERPARTY               JURISDICTION   RATING     STATUS      LAST SEEN
-------------------------  ------------   --------   ---------   ----------
Golden Sands Trading LLC   Dubai, UAE     Medium     Cleared     15 Mar 2025
  Reason: The customer informed the branch that the business was
  closing. All outstanding transactions were settled and the file was
  closed on 20 March 2025.

Al Furjan Jewellery Co     Sharjah, UAE   Medium     Cleared     22 May 2025
  Reason: The customer's trade licence expired and was not renewed.
  The relationship was closed after a 90-day grace period.

Horizon Metals FZE         RAK, UAE       High       Escalated   04 Jul 2025
  Reason: The compliance function identified a pattern of transactions
  consistent with the gold-for-cash typology. The MLRO reviewed the
  file and determined that the relationship should be terminated.
  A Suspicious Transaction Report was filed on 10 July 2025. The
  customer was informed of the termination by letter on 11 July 2025
  without disclosure of the filing.

Pacific Gold Traders Pte   Singapore      Medium     Cleared     18 Aug 2025
  Reason: The counterparty was acquired by a third party and the
  trading relationship was not continued under the new ownership.
  The file was closed on 01 September 2025.

Burj Al Arab Precious      Dubai, UAE     High       Escalated   02 Oct 2025
  Reason: The compliance function identified that the beneficial owner
  of the counterparty was added to the UAE Local Terrorist List on
  28 September 2025. The relationship was terminated immediately. A
  Funds Freeze Report was filed on 02 October 2025 and a Suspicious
  Activity Report was filed on 03 October 2025. The customer's assets
  held by the firm were frozen pending instruction from the Executive
  Office for Control and Non-Proliferation.

Dar Al Dhahab LLC          Abu Dhabi, UAE Low        Cleared     12 Nov 2025
  Reason: The customer ceased trading in precious metals and
  transitioned to a jewellery retail business that falls outside the
  firm's risk appetite for counterparty relationships. The file was
  closed by mutual agreement on 30 November 2025.
