                                                               Rating
                                                               ------

I understand that the Ministry of Economy reviews RBA          [    ]

I know that the FATF list of jurisdictions under increased     [    ]
monitoring and the FATF list of high-risk jurisdictions
subject to a call for action are used by the firm to
assess geographic risk in its counterparty population.
