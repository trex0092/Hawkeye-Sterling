2.  SUSPICIOUS TRANSACTION AND ACTIVITY REPORTS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                                               Rating
                                                               ------
I understand the difference between a Suspicious               [    ]
Transaction Report (STR) and a Suspicious Activity Report
(SAR), and I know when each type is appropriate.

I know that the firm's obligation to file an STR arises        [    ]
under Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025 and is aligned
with FATF Recommendation 20 (reporting of suspicious
transactions).

I understand that suspicion is a subjective standard and       [    ]
that I must report any transaction or activity that gives
me reasonable grounds to suspect it may be related to
money laundering or terrorism financing.

I know that the STR Quality Score report assesses each         [    ]
draft against a 12-point rubric and that the MLRO uses
this score to determine whether a draft is ready for
filing.

I understand that tipping off a customer about a               [    ]
suspicious report is a criminal offence under UAE law.
