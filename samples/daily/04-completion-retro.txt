1. PURPOSE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This note compares the list of priorities circulated at 09:00 this
morning with the compliance work that was actually closed in Asana
during the day, entity by entity. It is produced so that the MLRO and
the Board can see, on any given working day, how closely the firm's
stated priorities match the firm's actual output, and so that items
that slipped are carried over explicitly rather than quietly.

The retro is not a performance review of any individual. It is a
programme-level record of what happened today.
