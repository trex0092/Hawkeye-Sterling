4. HITS AND DISPOSITIONS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hit 1. Counterparty Al Safwa Metals Trading FZ LLC. Referred by Entity Alpha
in the course of the RF-R12 file. Query time 09:12. Screening engine
returned a 91 percent match against the Consolidated List entry Al Safwa
Metals. The list entry lacks discriminating data that would allow the
compliance function to confirm or rule out identity on its own record.
Disposition: partial match pending clearance. The transaction on the
underlying file has been suspended. A Partial Name Match Report
candidate review has been prepared and is in the MLRO's queue.
Responsible officer: Compliance Analyst on duty, Entity Alpha.

Hit 2. Walk-in visitor presenting as Mohammad Saleh Al Farid, Emirates
ID 784-1988-XXXXXXX-X. Referred by Entity Echo branch screening. Query
time 11:48. Screening engine returned an 82 percent match against a
Local Terrorist List entry. On review, the date of birth on the list
entry does not match the date of birth on the Emirates ID presented,
and the middle name on the Emirates ID is absent from the list entry.
Disposition: cleared as a false positive on the basis of two
discriminating data points. The visitor was served and the transaction
proceeded.
Responsible officer: Compliance Analyst on duty, Entity Echo.

Hit 3. Existing customer Karim Faisal Al Mutawa, Emirates ID
784-1970-XXXXXXX-X. Referred during the routine re-screening cycle.
Query time 16:03. Screening engine returned a 78 percent match against
a Consolidated List entry with a similar surname and a different
nationality. Disposition: cleared. The nationality on the Emirates ID
and the nationality on the list entry are incompatible, and the
matching algorithm picked up on the surname alone. The disposition has
been recorded on the customer file and the customer relationship
continues on its existing risk rating.
Responsible officer: Deputy MLRO.

Hit 4. New customer onboarding referred by Entity Bravo. Subject: Samira Noor
Al Jabari, proposed beneficial owner of a corporate customer applying
to trade with the firm. Query time 13:29. Screening engine returned no
hit. Recorded here for completeness because the onboarding sits on the
firm's internal high-risk pathway.
Responsible officer: Deputy MLRO.
