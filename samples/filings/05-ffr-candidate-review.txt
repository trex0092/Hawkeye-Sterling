1. STATUS OF THIS DOCUMENT
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a draft candidate review of a Funds Freeze Report, prepared by
the compliance function for the personal review of the Money Laundering
Reporting Officer. It is the most consequential draft the compliance
function can produce. It is to be treated as the highest priority on
the MLRO's desk the moment it lands and under no circumstances may the
underlying material or the underlying funds move while the draft is
open.

The draft is conditional. It has been prepared on the working
assumption that additional discriminating data received after the
Partial Name Match Report will confirm the identity of the
counterparty as a party listed on the UN Security Council Consolidated
List. If that assumption holds, this draft becomes live. If the
additional data instead clears the match, this draft is closed with
no action and the underlying Partial Name Match Report disposition is
recorded as cleared.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. ACTIONS TAKEN OR TO BE TAKEN IMMEDIATELY ON CONFIRMATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

On confirmation of the match, the compliance function will take the
following actions without delay and in any event within the applicable
timeline set by the targeted financial sanctions framework.

a. The physical material described in section 4 will remain in the
   branch safe under dual control. It will not be released to the
   counterparty or to any third party.

b. The proposed settlement of AED 164,000 will not be released.
   The corresponding internal hold will be converted into a freeze.

c. Any existing contract, purchase order, delivery note or other
   instrument connecting the firm to the counterparty will be
   suspended in writing.

d. The counterparty will be informed in writing that the firm is
   unable to continue the transaction and that the matter has been
   referred to the competent authority. The letter will be
   co-signed by the MLRO.

e. The branch will be instructed not to communicate any further
   information to the counterparty or to any person claiming to act
   on the counterparty's behalf beyond the letter at point d.

f. The firm's staff who handled any part of the relationship with
   the counterparty will be informed of the freeze and of the
   prohibition on any further engagement.

g. The Funds Freeze Report will be filed through the Financial
   Intelligence Unit on the goAML platform in the name of the MLRO
   with the underlying evidence attached.

h. The filing reference returned by goAML will be recorded on the
   Asana task and on this document, and the Entity Foxtrot RF-R12 file will
   be updated accordingly.

i. The cross-entity counterparty register will be updated to reflect
   the freeze. The Entity Alpha RF-R12 file, which tracks a related
   counterparty, will be reviewed on the same day to determine
   whether the same freeze applies to that counterparty.

7. REGULATORY BASIS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This candidate review is prepared on the basis of the applicable
targeted financial sanctions framework administered by the Executive
Office for Control and Non-Proliferation, which requires the firm to
freeze without delay any funds and economic resources that belong to
or are controlled by a person listed on the UN Security Council
Consolidated List or the UAE Local Terrorist List, and to report the
freeze to the Financial Intelligence Unit through the goAML platform.
The primary legal basis is the applicable provision of Federal
Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025 on Anti-Money Laundering and Countering the
Financing of Terrorism and Financing of Illegal Organisations.
