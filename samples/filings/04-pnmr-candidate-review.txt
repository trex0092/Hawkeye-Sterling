-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. GROUNDS FOR FILING
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

The compliance function cannot, on its own record, determine whether
the counterparty and the list entry are the same legal person. The
match score is high. The discriminating data on the list entry is
thin. In accordance with the firm's internal sanctions procedure and
with the applicable targeted financial sanctions framework, where a
match cannot be discharged the firm's obligation is to notify the
Financial Intelligence Unit and to suspend any pending settlement
with the counterparty until the match is cleared or confirmed.

The compliance function therefore recommends the filing of a Partial
Name Match Report.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. ACTIONS TAKEN TO DATE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Immediately on the match being returned by the screening engine the
compliance function placed the underlying transaction on hold. No
funds have been released to the counterparty. The physical material
has not moved. The Entity Foxtrot branch has been instructed to treat the
counterparty as restricted and to escalate any further communication
from the counterparty to the compliance function.

The counterparty has not been informed of the match. The counterparty
has been told that the transaction is on hold for routine documentary
verification in accordance with our internal procedures, and has been
invited to provide additional identifying information for the
beneficial owner of the counterparty. That additional information is
awaited.

The compliance function has also noted, for the record, that a
closely related counterparty under the same corporate suffix and a
similar trade licence range is currently under review on the Entity Alpha
RF-R12 file. The two reviews are treated as a single cross-entity
investigation track and progress on either file is communicated to
the other reviewer.
