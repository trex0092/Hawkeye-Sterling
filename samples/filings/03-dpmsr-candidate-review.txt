6. BASIS FOR THE REPORT
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

This candidate review is prepared on the basis of the positive
reporting obligation that applies to Dealers in Precious Metals and
Stones in respect of cash purchases, or linked series of cash
purchases, at or above the cash transaction threshold specified by
the DPMSR framework applicable to the firm.

The compliance function has not formed a suspicion of money laundering
against the subject person. This draft does not rely on any suspicion
and should not be confused with a Suspicious Transaction Report. The
purpose of this draft is to ensure the firm's positive reporting
obligation is discharged in a timely manner if the MLRO confirms that
the aggregate cash consideration of AED 64,000 in the rolling 30 day
window reaches the cash transaction threshold that applies to the
firm.

The compliance function is aware that the cash transaction threshold
applicable to the firm has not been verbatim confirmed to it and
therefore expressly asks the MLRO to verify the threshold before
filing. If the threshold has not been reached on the MLRO's
determination, this draft should be closed without filing and the
customer relationship should be maintained on the firm's ongoing
monitoring list.
